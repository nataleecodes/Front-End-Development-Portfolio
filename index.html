About Me

I fell in love with coding at an introductory JavaScript workshop in Santa Monica. The instructor was Esther Lee, Senior Software Development Lead at Hulu. It was a hot day and most people would have preferred to be on the beach, but Esther? She’s practically effervescent when she’s explaining how computers process algorithms. I was hooked. Instantly. I've never been more satisfied with my career and I owe it to her.

I aspire to carry her verve into every workplace. I'm eager to pursue new skills and master emerging technologies.

My background in technical writing helps me approach software development projects with a well-rounded perspective. In previous workplaces, I’ve had the pleasure of connecting with engineers, designers, project managers, marketing pros and CTOs. In every role I held as a communications professional, I was always the first to raise my hand to troubleshoot website issues. Now I am thrilled to be focusing on code full-time.

I’ve always liked helping people. As a web developer, I’m excited to help people share great ideas and new services that have the power to transform the way we live.
